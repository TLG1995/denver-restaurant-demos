Colorado's First Ethiopian Restaurant

Since 1985, The Ethiopian Restaurant has been introducing Denver to the rich, complex flavors of Ethiopian cuisine. We were the first — and we're still the standard.

Every dish is served on injera, our signature spongy sourdough flatbread made fresh daily. Tear off a piece, scoop up a bite of spicy doro wot or tender lamb tibs, and experience food the way it was meant to be shared.

Family-owned and operated for nearly 40 years, we serve traditional recipes passed down through generations. No shortcuts. No fusion. Just authentic Ethiopian food.
