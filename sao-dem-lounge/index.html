A Taste of Vietnam on Federal

Sao Dem Starry Night Lounge brings the comforting flavors of Vietnam to Denver's Federal Boulevard. Our kitchen serves authentic pho, rice plates, and Vietnamese favorites made the traditional way.

Whether you're craving a steaming bowl of pho on a cold Colorado evening or looking for a casual spot to gather with friends, Sao Dem delivers warmth in every bowl.

Family recipes. Fresh ingredients. A welcoming atmosphere that keeps you coming back.
