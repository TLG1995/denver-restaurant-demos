Ethiopian Soul on Havana Street

Geez Habesha brings the warm, communal spirit of Ethiopian dining to Aurora's vibrant Havana Street corridor.

Our kitchen prepares traditional dishes using time-honored recipes and the finest spices — from our aromatic berbere-spiced stews to our fresh, spongy injera baked in-house daily.

Come for the food, stay for the hand-brewed Ethiopian coffee. This is more than a meal — it's an experience.
